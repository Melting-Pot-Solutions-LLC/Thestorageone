U-Haul Moving Truck Rentals

Whether you’re tackling your first move or the tenth, U-Haul has the Right Equipment at the Lowest Cost for whatever your moving needs are. U-Haul has the largest selection of new trucks specifically designed for your upcoming household move.

What is Truck Sharing?

Truck sharing with U-Haul gives you access to a moving truck without the costs and responsibilities of owning one. When you take advantage of U-Haul truck sharingSM, you can rent just the right equipment for each specific move. There is no need for you to invest in a fleet of trucks to cover all size moves. We share the widest range of moving trucks, pickup trucks and cargo vans with you for all your hauling needs.

One-Way Truck Rentals

Use U-Haul truck rentals to help you with your one-way move. With over 20,000 U-Haul rental locations spread our across the US and Canada, you can bet there is a U-Haul location near you. This saves you time and money when planning a one-way move.

In-Town® Truck Rentals

With local moving truck rentals, you pick up and drop off at the same location. This makes it a convenient option for a local move or local delivery.
